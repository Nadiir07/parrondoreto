Our legacy speaks for us

The best Asturian cuisine in Madrid

Our History

For over 40 years, Casa Parrondo has been serving traditional Asturian dishes in the heart of Madrid. Our legacy spans generations, bringing authentic flavors and culinary techniques to each dish we serve.

Our Legacy

From the humble beginnings of our founder, Nicolás Parrondo, our restaurant has become a symbol of quality and authenticity, showcasing the best of Asturias in every bite.

Our Meals

Our menu features the finest tapas, freshly made every day with locally sourced ingredients. From patatas al Cabrales to traditional fabada, each dish represents the essence of Asturias.

Our Drinks

We offer the best selection of Asturian ciders, including the renowned Sidra Peñón, as well as a variety of local wines and refreshing beverages to complement your meal.

Our Location

Located in the heart of Madrid, Casa Parrondo is easy to find and offers a cozy atmosphere for all our guests. Come visit us to enjoy a true taste of Asturias.

Our Origins

Originating from the rich culinary traditions of Asturias, Casa Parrondo brings the flavors and customs of this beautiful region to Madrid. Our roots are deep in Asturian culture and gastronomy.
